Range

Using any vocal sound you find comfortable. go up and down the scales changing the keys as you go along Rate yourself on how clear your scales were and how many key climbs you were able to do.

Level 1 - I ascended up the scales but couldnt descend or change scale keys

Level 2 - I was able to ascend and decend through the scales on up to two keys

Level 3 - I was able to ascend, decend and randomly go through the scales. I was able to change the scale key a number of times

Tone

Using any vocal sound you find comfortable.

Perform a single note remaining on your most comfortable note for as long as you can.

Rate yoursef depending on how long you can hold a full sound.

Level 1 - Up to 10 seconds

Level 2 - Up to 15 seconds

Level 3 - Above 20 seconds

Rate yoursef on your abilty to perform the full sound and its clarity.

Level 1 - i couldnt make the siren sound

Level 2 - I was able to do one full sirent but couldnt replecated on another key.

Level 3 - I was able to do a siren using different vocal sounds ascending and decending across different keys.
